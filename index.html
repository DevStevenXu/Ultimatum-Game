The Ultimatum Game

Welcome! The following survey is based off of a popular game in economics.

If someone offered you money, would you take it? Easy choice right?

As you answer 6 quick questions, you will be making choices that
reflect how you respond in different circumstances.

The rules of the game are as follows:

  • You are participating with one other person. Let's call him Mike.
  • Mike will be given a sum of money.
  • Mike will decide how to spilt the money with you.
  • You will decide whether you want to accept the proposal.
  • If you accept, then the money will be split accordingly.
  • If not, then neither you nor Mike will receive any money.
